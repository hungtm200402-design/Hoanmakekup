# Backend/Admin Context

The backend/admin area supports business operations for Hoanmakekup.

## Purpose

Admins use backend/admin capabilities to manage appointments, makeup services, cosmetic products, orders, and customers.

## Core Concepts

- **Admin**: A staff user managing operational data and customer activity.
- **Appointment management**: Reviewing, updating, confirming, or cancelling makeup bookings.
- **Service management**: Creating and maintaining the makeup services offered to customers.
- **Product management**: Creating and maintaining cosmetic products and trusted product information.
- **Order management**: Reviewing and fulfilling customer orders.
- **Customer management**: Viewing and maintaining customer records relevant to bookings and orders.

## Notes For Agents

- Prefer admin-facing language when describing operational workflows.
- Keep service, product, appointment, order, and customer concepts distinct unless the code intentionally joins them.
- When backend changes affect customer flows, also read `docs/context/frontend.md`.
